About PTE Coaching

An Overview:
The pearson Test of English is a computer based academic English langugae test aimed at non-native English speakers wanting to study and settle abroad. It tests Reading, writing , listening and speaking skills. PTE academic is a multilevel test and results are available within five business days.

Time Taken Test takes approx three hours to complete.This includes an un-timed introduction to the test and one optional scheduled break of up to 10 mins. There are three parts timed parts: Speaking and writing, Reading , Listening.

Basic Information about PTE

Speciality

To complete a PTE Academic test, you will need to attend a secure Pearson test center. You will use a computer and headset to listen to, read and respond to questions.

Part 1: Speaking and Writing

" 77-93 mins "
Personal introduction, Read aloud, Repeat sentence, Describe image, Re-tell lecture, Answer short question, Summarize written text, Essay.

Part 2:Reading

"32-41 minutes"
Multiple choice-choose single answer, Multiple choice-choose multiple answers, Re-order paragraphs, Reading: Fill in the blanks, Reading n writing: fill in the blanks

Part 3:Listening

"45-57 miutes"
Summarize spoken text, Multiple choice questions, Fill in the blanks, Highlight the correct summary, Multiple choice questions, Select missing word, Highlight incorrect words, Write from dictation.
