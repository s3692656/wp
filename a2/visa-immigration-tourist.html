About Tourist Visa

If any of your family, relative or friends want to visit Australia, we can help you with Visitor Visa process. This visa lets you visit Australia to visit or for business visitor purposes, or for up to three, six or 12 months.

You might be able to get this visa if you are travelling to Australia: as a visitor, for business visitor activities, to visit family, on a tour with a registered travel agent from the People’s Republic of China.

Our counselors will also make you aware of the visitor visa regulations in your chosen country. Please contact your nearest counselor or complete an enquiry form for further details.
