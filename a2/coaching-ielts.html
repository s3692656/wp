About IELTS Coaching

An Overview:
Both IELTS Academic and IELTS General Training test your Listening, Reading, Writing and Speaking skills in English.The total test time is under three hours.

General Training is accredited for migration, this test measures practical, everyday English skills.Academic is for people planning to study in highrer educationor seeking professional registration.

How to Prepare for IELTS

Listening

In this module there are 40 questions and time allocated for it is approximately 30 minutes.

Reading

In this module there are 40 questions and time allocated for it is approximately 60 minutes.

Writing

In this module there are 2 tasks. First is some graph or chart, second is an essay. Time allocated for it is approximately 60 minutes for both tasks.

Speaking

In this module there are 3 parts.It starts with general introduction questions plus discussion on common topic. Then there is cue card topic for which candidates are given 2 minutes time to think and then speak. In the last part examiner will ask question on the same topics. It takes nearly 11 to 14 minutes.
